Who I am

An Atlanta-based software developer

I am Nicole Gathany, ATLien (shout out to Outkast) and public-health-servant-turned-community-organizer-turned-web-developer.

My mom says that ever since I was a little girl, I have always had two passions: numbers/problem solving and people.

I spent some time in public health and reproductive justice, but I always had the feeling that I was not maximizing my full potential.

I needed a skillset for a field of work that would combine my love for solving problems and my empathy for people.

For the past 8 years, I have built digital tools for public health organizations and ed tech start-ups mostly as a product engineer. Through this work I have witnessed the value of software engineers with empathy and I am so proud to be one of them.
